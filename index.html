About Us

Tyler dreamed of flying high in the media world – even as a kid. He was just 10 when he started his first venture: a monthly Christian bulletin called Tyler’s Bible Newsletter. He wrote, designed and distributed the periodical, which featured inspirational articles. And he’s been working toward his goals ever since.

A native of North Port, Florida, Tyler began interning for the local newspaper when he was 12. Just a few years later, he was freelancing. His passion for communications has grown from those early experiences.

Launch

Over the years, Eagle Marketing & Design has grown from a digital publishing firm to offer advertising and public relations support for a variety of clients. Our services include communications strategy, web and graphic design, internal and external communications, media relations, digital marketing and more.

The company was originally launched as Reflections Media back in 2016. Founder Tyler Gibson later changed its name to honor his grandfather Bob Aslinger, who had a deep love for the majestic birds. Take your communications to new heights with Eagle Marketing & Design.
